Introduction to P2P Trading

P2P trading allows individuals to trade directly with each other without intermediaries.

Key Terminologies

  • Escrow: A service that holds assets until the transaction is complete.
  • Liquidity: The ease with which an asset can be converted into cash.
  • Arbitrage: The practice of taking advantage of price differences in different markets.
